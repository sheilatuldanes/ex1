EFFECTIVE WEB-BASED FORM

With Bill Derouchey's article/slideshow, I had a better understanding of everday interfaces that utilizes some type of form. One form that I often use is the UC Davis Schedule Builder. As a student, I find it very useful. It is much easier to use than how students previously registered (which was through SisWeb). The website is really easy to navigate through because the buttons that you really need is seen on the top of the page. When you click on a button, say "Add/Search Courses", a form shows up with specific inputs that narrows the options. However, one criticism that I have is that the website can be a bit busy. There is a lot of text surrounding the course schedule.
